What is

Django Girls is a non-profit organization and a community that empowers and helps women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources and support.

Workshops have taken place all over the world, including right here in Kansas City.

Last Year

In 2016, we hosted our first Django Girls in Kansas City. With the help of 16 mentors, we introduced nearly 100 women to web development through Django.

How to get involved

Apply to Attend

Women 14+ with little to no programming experience are encouraged to apply - djangogirls.org/kansascity/apply

Coaches will be available to help with any issues during the workshop and meals are provided

Attendees need a Windows or Mac laptop computer

Participants can bring an iPad or tablet to read through the tutorial during the event, but they are not recommended tools for programming.

Be A Coach

People of all genders are welcome to sign up as a coach - bit.ly/DjangoGirlsKCVolunteer

You do not need to be Django/Python developer to be a coach

Coaches will need a good understanding of basic programming fundamentals and the ability to debug technical issues

You can review the tutorial here
